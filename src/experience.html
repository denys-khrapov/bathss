Benefits of Hot and Cold Contrast Therapy

1

Heat Shock Proteins and Immune System Boost

The Banya experience of extreme hot and cold exposure helps to produce heat shock proteins, which support a healthy immune system. Studies have even shown that these proteins help to reduce the body’s long-term stress response and decrease inflammation – helping with muscle and joint pain and even reducing the symptoms of depression and anxiety.

2

Cardiovascular Health and Circulation

By heating the body to a high temperature and then cooling it down, a traditional Russian Banya experience causes the heart to beat faster in the steam room before slowing right down in the cool plunge pool. This process strengthens the heart muscles, stimulates blood vessels and can help protect against certain serious cardiovascular health conditions. WARNING: Please consult your doctor if you have chronic cardiovascular conditions before using hot and cold therapy.

3

Stress Relief

Stress causes an increase of norepinephrine (adrenaline), epinephrine and dopamine in the brain and body. Deliberate cold exposure has the same impact – but without the worry or anxiety, meaning it helps train the body to learn to combat and control stress in a calm, relaxing environment - maintaining mental clarity in the process. By improving mental resilience through these rituals, we can also increase mental acuity and the ability to focus.

4

Healthy Sleep and Jetlag Recovery

Jetlag disrupts the body’s natural circadian rhythms – which impacts sleep patterns. Changing your core body temperature through intense cold exposure helps to reset these rhythms, promoting restful sleep. The intense cold of the plunge pool or bucket shower also helps release adrenaline and stress hormones that help combat unwanted travel tiredness or lethargy – while the increased oxygen levels in the blood help to reduce the aches and pains that can follow a long flight.

5

Mental Clarity

Intense heat therapy helps promote a feeling of mental calm and relaxation, and can even help ease conditions such as depression and anxiety. Heat exposure helps produce endorphins and hermetic stress hormones (sometimes called ‘good stress’) which help to decrease the body’s stress sensitivity and increase tolerance to everyday stress. Outside of that, the ritualistic nature of the Banya and the self-reflective process allow for an almost meditative experience that roots you in time and place, away from everyday stress.

6

Post-Workout and Injury Recovery

Hot and cold contrast reduces your lactic acid build-up and the increased levels of oxygen in the blood, caused by the increase of heart rate in the steam room, help muscles recover quicker and muscle injuries heal faster.

7

Boosting Metabolism

Cold water therapy and exposure helps to convert white fat cells into more metabolically and thermogenically active beige and brown fat cells – allowing us to burn more calories.

8

Weight Loss and Detoxing

The intense steam and heat opens the pores on the skin - helping expel toxins and dead skin cells and reducing the signs of ageing. In turn - studies have shown that Banya and sauna also promote healthy blood flow and oxygenation, which can help maintain a healthy weight (when combined with a healthy diet and regular exercise).

9

Respiratory Conditions and Colds

Steam and aromatherapy treatments help open up the sinuses and promote healthy breathing and vascular elasticity. In addition, the heat on the skin and the increase in body temperature can help to simulate a ‘fever’ - which provides your immune system with a strength workout that will pay dividends when called on for real.
