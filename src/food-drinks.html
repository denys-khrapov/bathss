FAQ

Should I order food/drink before, during or after my treatment?

You can order food and drink at any point during your Banya No. 1 – Hoxton experience – however we do not recommend eating large portions or drinking alcoholic beverages immediately before or between your thermal treatments. Alcohol and larger dishes such as crayfish platters or meat dishes are best enjoyed in your final hour in the spa – following completion of your spa treatments – allowing you to enjoy the comforting dishes and cosy atmosphere, while basking in your post-Parenie glow.
